
## Change Browser Tab Title to Flowsert

### Summary
Update the browser tab title from "Lovable App" to "Flowsert" so the tab displays your brand name correctly.

### Changes Required

**File: `index.html`**
- Change `<title>Lovable App</title>` to `<title>Flowsert</title>`
- Update `<meta property="og:title">` from "Lovable App" to "Flowsert"
- Update `<meta name="description">` to reflect Flowsert
- Update `<meta property="og:description">` to reflect Flowsert  
- Update `<meta name="author">` from "Lovable" to "Flowsert"

### Technical Details
The title tag in `index.html` controls what appears in the browser tab. The OpenGraph (og:) meta tags control how your site appears when shared on social media platforms like Facebook and LinkedIn.

### Result
After this change, the browser tab will display "Flowsert" instead of "Lovable App", and social sharing previews will also show the correct branding.
